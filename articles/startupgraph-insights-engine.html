Get Off the Crunchbase Treadmill

Startup Intelligence Data Real-time

Everyone thinks Crunchbase is the gold standard for startup intelligence.

If you’re a founder or investor, you’re stuck on the Crunchbase treadmill:

Wait for the alert → See the news → Research the company → Decide.

Here’s the catch: By the time it hits Crunchbase, the outreach window is closed.

The real signal doesn’t appear in a funding alert. It’s buried in the patterns of hiring velocity, product velocity, and tech-stack changes.

Startup intelligence is currently broken. It’s backward-looking, siloed, and expensive.

We don't need another database. We need an intelligence layer.

We need modular, self-hosted engines that convert noise into real-time signal:

🚀 Funding Pattern Matcher: Finds companies nearing their next milestone — not the ones that already closed.

📈 Hiring Heatmap: Detects scaling inflection points (like the first engineering manager hire) months in advance.

⚡ Momentum Radar: Correlates product launches, office moves, and executive changes to spot peak-change windows.

This is how you spot opportunity before it hits the news.

Stop staring at the rearview mirror. Build an intelligence stack that sees around corners.

Get off the treadmill.
