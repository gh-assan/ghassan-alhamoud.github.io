EchoWeave: Your Phone Is a Graveyard. Let's Make It a Storyteller.

Privacy On-Device AI Personal OS

Your phone is stealing your life.

It knows your health stats, your schedule, every message you’ve sent, and every photo you’ve taken.

But it’s also a graveyard. All that data is siloed, locked behind apps, and essentially dead.

Every day your data sits locked, you lose a piece of your own history.

We’ve been sold the lie that more data equals better insights. But analytics dashboards are cold. They show you numbers, not your life.

What if your phone didn't just store your data — but told your story?

Enter EchoWeave: An on-device personal narrative operating system.

It’s built on three core pillars:

  1. Storyline: A private, automatic journal weaving together your health, calendar, and messages into a coherent temporal narrative.
  2. Weave: Proactive insights that connect the dots. Not just "you walked 5,000 steps," but "you go to this café before every major meeting — want me to pre-order your coffee?"
  3. Agentic Weave: It doesn't just watch. It acts. Want to reschedule a haircut to a day when you’re free? It does it.

The most important part? It’s 100% on-device. No cloud. No data harvesting. Your life remains entirely yours.

Think about it: in 20 years, do you want a dashboard of your life, or the story of it?

The era of the "Quantified Self" was just the beginning. The era of the "Narrative Self" is here.
