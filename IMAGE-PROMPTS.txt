PHOTOGRAPHER BRIEF:
  Environment: Natural or semi-natural setting that reads calm and
    grounded. Options in order of preference:
      — Outdoors: dappled light under trees, a garden wall, textured
        stone or weathered wood in soft focus behind you.
      — Indoors: large window with diffused daylight, a living wall
        or bookshelf, warm concrete or raw linen backdrop.
    Avoid: white studio cyclorama, dark dramatic backgrounds,
    corporate boardroom, busy street.
  Lighting: Soft natural light, ideally overcast or north-facing
    window. No harsh shadows. Slight warmth (golden hour or
    warm-balanced interior). Think editorial, not corporate.
  Wardrobe: Clean, understated. Dark navy, forest green, deep
    charcoal, or natural linen shirt. No patterns, no logos.
    Collarless or minimal collar. Communicates thoughtful and calm.
  Expression: Engaged and direct — slight half-smile is fine.
    Should read as someone you'd trust with a complex problem.
    Not stiff, not over-casual.
  Post-processing: Airy, natural. Slight lift in shadows.
    Preserve skin tones, don’t over-sharpen. Gentle desaturation
    is fine, but keep warmth. Filmic grain optional.
  Aspect ratio: 4:5 portrait — shoulders to just above head,
    ~20% headroom, centered.
