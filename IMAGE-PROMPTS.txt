PHOTOGRAPHER BRIEF:
  Environment: Studio or clean architectural interior (concrete
    wall, minimal backdrop). No busy backgrounds.
  Lighting: Broad key light from 45° camera-left, soft fill from
    right. Small rim light behind to separate subject from dark
    background. Think editorial / tech exec look, not corporate
    headshot.
  Wardrobe: Dark collarless shirt or minimal crew-neck (no tie,
    no loud patterns). Communicates technical credibility + modern.
  Expression: Direct eye contact, slight confident half-smile.
    Not stiff. Not grinning. Thinking-but-approachable.
  Post-processing: Dark, filmic, slight desaturation.
    Skin tones warm but not orange. Contrast is high.
    No heavy retouching — credibility over perfection.
  Aspect ratio: 4:5 portrait (tight crop above shoulders to
    mid-chest, centered, with ~20% head room above).
  If using AI (e.g. for a placeholder): describe as a Middle
    Eastern man, early 40s, serious tech-exec energy.
