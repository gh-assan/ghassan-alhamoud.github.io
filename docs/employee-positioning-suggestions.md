# Employee Positioning Suggestions

Goal: make the site read as a senior engineering portfolio, not a consulting funnel.

## Current Change

- Hide booking CTAs, contact CTAs, article CTAs, and availability badges with CSS.
- Keep the HTML intact so the consulting version can be restored quickly later.
- Avoid showing "currently accepting clients" or "book a free call" while applying for employee roles.

## Recommended Next Changes

1. Reframe the hero.
   - Current: "I help engineering teams..."
   - Better: "Senior Software Engineer focused on AI systems, distributed architecture, and production reliability."
   - Reason: it signals capability without sounding like a vendor pitch.

2. Rename "Services" to "Expertise".
   - Suggested sections: AI Systems, Distributed Systems, Architecture, Reliability, Engineering Leadership.
   - Reason: employers scan for strengths, not purchasable services.

3. Rename "Work" to "Selected Work" or "Case Studies".
   - Keep proof, outcomes, constraints, and technical decisions.
   - Remove sales language such as pricing, engagement, discovery call, and retainer.

4. Convert the process section into "How I Work".
   - Replace "30-min Intro Call" and "Clear Scoping" with collaboration style.
   - Example: problem framing, system design, implementation, mentoring, operational follow-through.

5. Add a resume-oriented page.
   - Include roles, companies, technologies, selected achievements, and links to articles/projects.
   - Keep it concise and easy to print or export.

6. Keep contact available but neutral later.
   - A simple email or LinkedIn link is fine.
   - Avoid urgency, availability claims, and meeting-booking language.

7. Tighten article CTAs.
   - Replace "Get in Touch" blocks with "Read related work" or remove them.
   - Reason: articles should showcase judgment, not push lead generation.

8. Adjust footer language.
   - Current: "AI Architecture Consulting — Worldwide"
   - Better: "Senior Software Engineer — AI Systems and Architecture"

## Strongest Positioning Angle

"I build production-grade AI and distributed systems, with enough architecture depth to design them and enough implementation depth to ship them."

This keeps the site aligned with employee roles while preserving the senior consulting-quality proof already present.
